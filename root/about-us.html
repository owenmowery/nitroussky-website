About us

Our Mission:

To provide entirely carbon neutral delivery and transportation services.

Nitrous Sky was founded in 2020 to build the world's first entirely carbon neutral delivery and transportation service. Our custom software platforms enable both businesses and consumers to get the items they need delivered, when they need them.

Need something delivered to you or shipped from you? Whether it´s delivery of pharmacy prescriptions, clothing, flowers, laundry, electronics or something else, we´ll do it. On demand, same day, whenever you need it, we´ll deliver it. Our delivery fleet uses electric and hybrid electric vehicles with plans to expand into electric vans, electric mopeds, drones and more. .

We live in an on demand world and a convenience driven world. Whether you’re a business owner or an individual consumer, you know the pains of inconvenience. You can’t afford the pains of slow delivery or the costs of expensive delivery services. We provide quicker, more convenient and cleaner delivery of items. Say goodbye to next day delivery and say hello to next episode delivery. Nitrous Sky´s delivery will help you save your time, money and stress with simple ordering.

Why wait around for a cleaner future when we can start now? The problem is that transport has a huge carbon footprint. The solution is combining clean transport modes with powerful technology. We free up people to do what they enjoy, not spend their time worrying about deliveries. Nitrous Sky will help you do that. Let's build a cleaner future together.
