About us

Our Mission:

To create entirely carbon neutral and contact-less supply chains.

Nitrous Sky was founded in 2020 to build the world's first entirely carbon neutral delivery and transportation service. Our custom software platforms enable both businesses and consumers to get the items they need, when they need them.

Need something delivered to you or shipped from you? We'll do it. Our fleet uses electric and hybrid electric vehicles, electric vans, electric mopeds, drones and more. We are testing drones for short range cargo delivery, to be launched shortly.

Why wait around for a cleaner future when we can start now? The problem is that transport has a huge carbon footprint. The solution is combining clean transport modes with powerful technology. We free up people to do what they enjoy, not spend their time worrying about their supply chain. Nitrous Sky will help you do that. Let's build a cleaner future together.
